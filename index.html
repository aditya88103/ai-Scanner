NutriScan
Scan a barcode to pull product data from Open Food Facts and estimate a simple nutrition score (0-10). This is not medical advice. Always check the label and your dietary needs.

Features
Designed to be fast, simple, and useful while shopping.
Quick barcode scan
Uses the fastest scanner available on your device.
Flashlight toggle
Turn on the torch in low light (when supported).
Nutrition score (0–10)
Simple, explainable score from available nutrition data.
Local history
Your recent scans stay on your device (no account).
Cleaner details
Only shows fields that are actually available for the product.
